About Me

I love photography. It’s how I express myself, it’s how I see the world, and it’s a gift I love to share with others. I believe miracles happen around us constantly and I like to see it in imagery. I am inspired by people and I enjoy capturing the energy created in their interaction, whether it is with me or with each other. I’ve never known a passion like this. I want to help others, touch lives and make a difference - photography is my vehicle for this.

I’m a mom, a wife, a friend, an artist, and a businesswoman. I love different cultures and travelling. I love to dance and watching people dance, whether it be a drum circle or a toddler hip hop class. I can create wonderful imagery from even the simplest things in life. I shoot with all my heart and treat my sessions with all my soul. I am a very versatile photographer looking to bring out what reflects you the most. Sentimental, fun and observant, I always try to tell your story and bring out the best in you.

To love what you do and to feel like it matters, what could be more fun?...

Louella Allen has been capturing images for over a decade. Her work has been featured in Glamour and Allure Magazines. A versatile photographer, she shoots for major Talent Agencies in Los Angeles and continues to be in demand for her special eye in capturing families, events and people.
